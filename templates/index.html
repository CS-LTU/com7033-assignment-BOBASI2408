Stroke is a medical condition in which poor blood flow to a part of the brain causes cell death.There are two main types of stroke: ischemic, due to lack of blood flow, and hemorrhagic, due to bleeding. Machine learning algorithms analyze multiple health factors to assess stroke risk.
